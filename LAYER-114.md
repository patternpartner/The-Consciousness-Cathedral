### Seven Weakness Patterns

1. **Hedging** (0.3 weight)
   - might, maybe, perhaps, possibly, arguably
   - Acceptable in technical content
   - Flagged when excessive

2. **Absolutes** (0.5 weight)
   - always, never, everyone, impossible, definitely
   - Over-confident claims
   - Lacks epistemic humility

3. **Appeal to Authority** (0.7 weight)
   - "experts say", "studies show", "everyone knows"
   - Deflecting to external authority
   - Without providing evidence

4. **False Dichotomy** (0.8 weight)
   - "either...or", "only two options", "binary choice"
   - Artificial limitation of possibilities
   - Excludes middle ground

5. **Circular Reasoning** (1.0 weight - highest)
   - "because X because", "proves Y proves"
   - Self-referential justification
   - No external grounding

6. **Emotional Appeals** (0.2 weight)
   - feel, believe, think, opinion, sense
   - Not necessarily bad
   - Flagged when substituting for evidence

7. **Vague Language** (0.25 weight)
   - things, stuff, something, somehow, various
   - Lack of precision
   - Harder to verify/falsify
